About Us

At CulturalTreasures, we are dedicated to showcasing and preserving cultural artifacts from around the globe. Our mission is to educate and inspire through the beauty of diverse cultures.

We believe that understanding and appreciating different cultures enriches our lives and fosters a sense of community. Our team works tirelessly to bring you exhibitions, educational programs, and resources that celebrate the world's cultural heritage.

Delivery Policy

We strive to ensure that your order is delivered promptly. Orders are typically processed within 1-3 business days. Delivery times may vary based on your location and the shipping method selected at checkout.

  • Shipping Methods: We offer various shipping options, including standard and expedited shipping.
  • Tracking: Once your order has shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email to monitor your shipment's progress.
  • International Shipping: We do offer international shipping; however, delivery times may vary significantly based on customs and local postal services.

Return/Refund Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not satisfied, you may return eligible items within 14 days of receiving your order.

  • Conditions for Returns: Items must be unused, in their original packaging, and accompanied by a receipt or proof of purchase.
  • Return Process: To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team for instructions. Do not return items without prior authorization.

Refunds: Refunds will be processed to the original payment method once the returned items are received and inspected.

  • Return Shipping Costs: Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or incorrect.
  • Processing Time: Please allow 5-7 business days for the refund to reflect in your account after we process it.

Damaged or Defective Items: If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately with photos and a description of the issue. We will work with you to resolve the matter promptly.
